# Title: OSINT Exercise #004 - This is a photo of a resort located on an island.

## Source: https://gralhix.com/list-of-osint-exercises

**Objective:**

* a) What is the name of the resort?
* b) What are the coordinates of the island?
* c) In which cardinal direction was the camera facing when the photo was taken?

# 1. Initial Observation

**Visual landmarks:** 
* An island, very round island, I can see other islands and one in the background has a mountain
* Guessing its a resort island
* Kyaks on the beach, specifically 2

**Initial Hypothesis:** 

I'd like to think its somewhere near the Hawaii area, I feel like thats the usual place with islands in clusters that provide resorts.

# 2. The Investigation

* Step 1:
  * Google Lens: Reverse search the island picture. Found islands with a similar bay look that are resorts
* Step 2: 
  * Google Search: Found a website with the exact picture we were given and even more angles of the island. I presume its the correct island named Oan

![Evidence 01](./assets/.png)

* Step 3:
  * Google Maps 3D verification: Found the island and found the exact angle to get the 3 islands and the mountainy mountain on the edge of the photo
![Evidence 02](./assets/.png)
* Step 4:
  * Google Maps Compass: Got the angle of the picture and got the cardinal direction
![Evidence 03](./assets/.png)

# 3. The Solution

* A) Oan Resort
* B) Coordinates: 7.362594701876925, 151.75627518762508
* C) North-East

# 4. Tools Utilized

* Search Engines: Google (Searching), Google Maps (Visual), Google Lens (Reverse searching)
* Environment: Arch linux / Brave
